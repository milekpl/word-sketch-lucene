## Known Limitations (v1.0)

### What's NOT in this release
- ❌ Agreement rules (no noun-adjective matching)
- ❌ More than 4 grammatical relations
- ❌ Advanced morphological analysis
- ❌ Word sense disambiguation

These are acceptable limitations for v1.0 and planned for v2.0.
